About

Your Division 8 partner in Atlantic Canada.

We represent the industry's leading door, frame, glass and sealing manufacturers — and put that engineering to work on your project.

Atlantic Entry Solutions is the authorized manufacturers' representative for Division 8 commercial openings and hardware across the Maritimes and Newfoundland & Labrador. We are the single point of contact between architects, contractors and distributors and the factories that build the products.

We don't manufacture and we don't compete with your distributor. Our role is to specify the opening correctly — the right core, the right rating, the right sealing — and then route the project to the authorized stocking distributor who fulfills it. That protects the supply chain, the specification, and the architect's intent.

What we represent

Architectural wood doors (Lambton), hollow-metal doors and frames (DeLa Fontaine), building-envelope sealing and vision lites (Unique Architectural, National Guard Products, Draftseal), and fire-rated architectural glass (Glassopolis) — with fire and acoustic assemblies engineered across all of them.

Specification support

Fire-code validation, rating selection and substitutions — grounded in the applicable code, not a catalog.

Local routing

We protect the supply chain by routing projects to authorized, vetted stocking distributors in your territory.

Manufacturer depth

Represented lines spanning wood, steel, glass, louvers, sliding hardware and sealing — engineered to work together in one opening.

Territory

Serving all of Atlantic Canada
