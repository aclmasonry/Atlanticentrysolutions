Glassopolis

Canada's largest fire-rated glass specialist — SCHOTT PYRAN Platinum glass-ceramic and Protect3 impact-safe fire glazing, cut and shipped fast.

Fire-rated glazing Impact-safe PYRAN Platinum Protect3 X-ray shielding

Glassopolis is Canada’s fire-rated glass specialist — the glazing that turns a vision lite into a certified fire and life-safety assembly. The line leads with SCHOTT PYRAN Platinum, a fire-rated glass-ceramic, and Protect3, a filmed product that is impact-safe as well as fire-rated.

That distinction matters on a real opening: fire-rated glass and impact-safe glass are two separate requirements, and a lite in a door often has to satisfy both. Choosing a product that carries both avoids discovering the gap at inspection.

Pre-installed in NGP’s low-profile vision-lite frames, Glassopolis glazing arrives as one assembly rather than a glass order and a frame order — ready to go into a Lambton wood or DeLa Fontaine steel opening.

Each product is listed for specific assemblies, thicknesses and sizes, so the current spec sheet is what to build a submittal from: Glassopolis product spec sheets (opens manufacturer site in a new tab) .

Atlantic Entry Solutions represents Glassopolis across Atlantic Canada for fire-rated vision lites and specialty architectural glass.

Key products

  • PYRAN Platinum — SCHOTT fire-rated glass-ceramic for doors and lites
  • Protect3 — fire-rated glazing that is also impact-safe
  • TEMP20 — clear fire-rated tempered glass for doors
  • ProtectX — lead X-ray radiation shielding glass
  • Protect FE (forced entry) and Protect WS (windstorm) security glass
