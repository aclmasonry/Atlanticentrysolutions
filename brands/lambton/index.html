Lambton Door

Canadian-made premium architectural wood doors and jambs — five-ply commercial and institutional openings with book-matched veneers, MDO and HPDL faces.

Fire-rated Acoustic-rated Lead-lined Bullet-resistant Oversized

Lambton Door builds premium architectural wood openings for commercial and institutional projects — the doors specifiers reach for when the finished face matters as much as the performance behind it. Five-ply construction supports an almost infinite range of face treatments, from book-matched natural veneers to MDO and high-pressure decorative laminate.

Where a project needs more than looks, Lambton’s dual-performance models pair a labeled fire core with an acoustic construction in the same leaf — so one door can answer both the fire code and the sound spec instead of forcing a trade-off between them. Lead-lined and bullet-resistant options cover healthcare imaging and security work, and jambs can be supplied to match.

Which core, face and size combinations carry a current label — and at what level — is set by Lambton’s certified schedule, which is the document to check against your opening schedule: Lambton’s Technical Space (opens manufacturer site in a new tab) .

Atlantic Entry Solutions specifies and coordinates Lambton openings across the Maritimes and connects your project to the authorized stocking distributor.
