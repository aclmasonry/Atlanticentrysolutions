For Manufacturers

Represent your line in Atlantic Canada.

We are a technical agency for Division 8 openings and hardware across the Maritimes — carrying a deliberately complementary, non-competing portfolio, and representing every principal in it properly.

Territory & reach

The whole region, one agency.

Atlantic Entry Solutions covers the Maritimes end to end — architectural firms, contract hardware houses, general contractors and institutional owners. One agency across the region means a principal gets consistent coverage rather than a patchwork of part-time attention.

The current portfolio

Eight lines that work together, not against each other.

The first thing a manufacturer checks is conflict. Our 8 lines are chosen to complete an opening rather than compete inside it — wood doors, hollow metal, architectural glass, vision frames and louvers, perimeter sealing, thresholds and sliding hardware. Several are already family: Anemostat operates under National Guard Products, Draftseal is an NGP company, and KrisTrack was formed to complement the Draftseal line.

A new line is only added where it rounds out the assembly and does not put an existing principal in a bidding war with a stablemate.

How we represent

What the agency does for a principal.

  • Specification calls

    Architects and spec writers across the Maritimes, working your line into project manuals before the tender goes out.

  • Distributor & estimator support

    Contract hardware houses quoted quickly and accurately, so your product gets bid instead of substituted out on a deadline.

  • Product knowledge sessions

    In-person and remote training that puts your line in front of the people who specify, quote and install it.

  • Field & project coordination

    Shipment tracing, timeline coordination and job-site follow-through — the factory represented properly, all the way to the opening.

Considering the Maritimes?

If you manufacture for Division 8 openings and are reviewing representation in Atlantic Canada — or are not currently sold here at all — tell us about the line. We will be straight with you about whether it fits the portfolio and where it would sell.
